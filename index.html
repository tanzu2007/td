A request to parents:-
Please contact the class teacher regularly to know your child’s overall performance and attendance. Before coming to meet your child’s class teacher, kindly check if he/she is free by calling him/her. If your child is in the class when you come to meet him/her please inform the principal and get the permission. Please be in formal dress when coming to the school. Students and teachers will answer phone calls during the break time only. From the time your child is dispersed from the school till he /she reports to the school the next day, it is the responsibility of the parents to look after him/her. If your child is unable to come to school due to religious functions or ill health, please phone and write your consent on parents’ consent form in the school diary stating the reason for your child’s absence from school. If you have any doubts, questions or remarks to make to the school management, please contact us at Phone/Fax No. 02-361304, Post Box No. 455, VP’s Office: 02-361200 or write to us on our email address kuzhugchenMSS@gmail.com
